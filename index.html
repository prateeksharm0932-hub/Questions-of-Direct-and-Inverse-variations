Que.1 A dozen notebooks cost ₹ 186.(i)What is the cost of 8 such notebooks?(ii)How many notebooks will cost ₹356.50?

(i)1 dozen notebooks=12 notebooks

SO,we can say the cost of 12 notebooks is ₹ 186.

(Unitary method) Now, the cost of 1 notebook= ₹186 12 =₹15.5

Here,the cost of 8 notebooks=15.5×8=₹124

The cost of 8 notebooks is ₹124.

(ii) (Unitary method) Cost of 1 notebook=₹15.5

Here,such a notebook can be bought for ₹356.50= ₹356.50 ₹15.5 =23 Notebooks

Que.11 25 men working 8 hours a day do a job in 63 days.How long would 45 men take to finish the job,working 7 hours a day?

25 men working 8 hours do a job in 63 days.

(Unitary method)∴ 25 men working 1 hour a day do a job in=63×8=504 Days

(Less hours=more days)

∴ 1 man working 1 hour a day does the job in=504×25 days

(less men = more days)

∴ 1 man working 7 hours a day does the job in= 504×25 7 days

(more work=less days)

45 men working 7 hours a day will do the job in= 504×25 7×45 days =40 days

(more men=less days)

Que.12 19 men working 7 1 2 hours a day can finish a job in 24 days.How many hours a day must 45 men work to complete it in 7 days?

step 1:Set up the table

Let the required hours be a.

Men Days Hours/Day
19 21 7.5
45 7 a
Step 2: Determine arrow directions.

° Men vs. hours: More men (19 to 45) will need fewer hours to do the same work.

(Inverse variation → opposite arrows)

° Days vs. hours: Fewer days (21 to 7) will need more hours to finish the same work.

(Inverse variation → Opposite arrows)

Step 3: Follow the arrows to create the ratios:

a=7.5 × ( 19 45 ) × ( 21 7 ) =9 1 2 hours

Again,4 women's earnings in a day=₹180

∴ 1 woman's earnings in a day= ₹180 4 =₹45

∴ 12 women's earnings in a day=₹45×12=₹540
